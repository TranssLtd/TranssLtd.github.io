Why Malindi?

Malindi is a coastal gem known for its unique mix of culture, history, and breathtaking landscapes. From the serene beaches of Watamu to the intriguing ruins of Gede, Malindi offers experiences that cater to every traveler's interests.

Your 3-Day Malindi Itinerary

Day 1: Discovering Malindi's Heritage

Begin your journey at the historic Gede Ruins, an ancient Swahili village surrounded by lush forests. Walk through its stone walls, mosques, and houses while uncovering stories of the past. Afterward, visit the Vasco da Gama Pillar, one of the oldest European monuments in Africa. Conclude your day at Malindi Museum, where you'll learn about the region's rich cultural and maritime history.

Day 2: Marine Adventures

Spend the day at Malindi Marine National Park, home to vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. Snorkel or dive to witness the underwater beauty, or take a glass-bottom boat ride if you prefer staying dry. Enjoy a picnic lunch at Watamu Beach, famous for its golden sands and calm turquoise waters. End your day with a sunset dhow cruise, soaking in the serene beauty of the Indian Ocean.
