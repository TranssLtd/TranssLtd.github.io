Itinerary: Discover Seven Forks

Join us for an unforgettable adventure as we explore the hidden gems of Seven Forks. Here’s the plan for the day:

8:00 AM

Departure from Nairobi

Begin your journey with a comfortable drive from Nairobi to the stunning landscapes of Seven Forks.

10:00 AM

Arrival at Seven Forks Dam

Enjoy the scenic beauty of the dam, learn about its history, and capture breathtaking photos.

12:00 PM

Guided Nature Walk

Embark on a guided walk through lush trails, discovering local flora and fauna.

1:30 PM

Lunch by the River

Relish a delicious meal by the riverside, surrounded by serene views and the sounds of nature.

3:00 PM

Kayaking and Adventure

Dive into thrilling activities like kayaking or opt for a peaceful boat ride on the river.

5:00 PM

Return to Nairobi

Conclude the day with a scenic drive back to Nairobi, taking home cherished memories.
