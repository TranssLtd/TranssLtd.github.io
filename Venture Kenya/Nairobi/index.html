Explore Nairobi National Park

Nestled just outside Kenya’s bustling capital, Nairobi National Park offers a one-of-a-kind safari experience. Known as “The World’s Wildlife Capital,” this park is home to a rich variety of animals, including lions, rhinos, giraffes, and over 400 bird species, all set against a stunning cityscape backdrop.

Abundant Wildlife

Get up close and personal with lions, rhinos, leopards, buffalo, and more in their natural habitat.

Unique Location

Experience a rare blend of urban life and wilderness, just a short drive from Nairobi’s city center.

Birdwatcher’s Paradise

Discover over 400 species of birds, making the park a top destination for bird enthusiasts.
