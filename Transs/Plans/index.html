Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our frequently asked questions by our customers.

  • What is the typical timeline for completing a website?

    Our standard timeline for a basic portfolio website is 6-12 hours. More complex projects, such as business or advanced business websites, can take 1-3 days depending on the specific requirements.

  • Do you offer maintenance services after the website is completed?

    Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your website remains up-to-date and secure.

  • Can I update the content on my website myself after it’s built?

    Absolutely! We use user-friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update your website’s content. We also provide training on how to use these systems.

  • Do you provide hosting and domain registration services?

    While we don't directly offer hosting and domain registration, we can recommend reliable providers and assist with the setup process.

  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    Yes, all our websites are designed to be fully responsive, ensuring they look great and function well on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.
